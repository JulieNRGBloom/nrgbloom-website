The Partnership Model

We Buy Your Gas. You Reduce Flaring. Both Sides Earn.

NRG Bloom enters a long-term community based power purchase agreement with the energy producer. We deploy, own, and operate all compute infrastructure on-site. The energy producer sells gas that would otherwise be flared — generating revenue from a stranded asset while earning quantified ESG impact.

Our current deployment in Nigeria operates under a community based power purchase agreement at Ogboinbiri, Bayelsa State — live since February 2025.
