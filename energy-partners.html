Your Stranded Gas. Our Compute Infrastructure. A Shared Revenue Model.

NRG Bloom provides oil and gas operators with a turnkey, zero-CapEx solution for monetizing stranded, associated, or penalized gas at the wellhead. We deploy our own infrastructure, purchase gas under a long-term Power Purchase Agreement, and convert it into high-performance compute, eliminating flaring liabilities while creating a new revenue stream from your existing operations.

What We Offer Producers

  • 24/7 Gas Offtake: We are the buyer of last resort. If you have gas, we have demand. Guaranteed, around the clock.
  • Zero CapEx to You: NRG Bloom finances, deploys, and maintains all compute infrastructure on your site.
  • Quantified ESG Impact: Every cubic meter of gas we consume is metered and reported as methane mitigated. We provide audit-ready documentation for your ESG disclosures and regulatory compliance.
  • Revenue Sharing: Structured PPA with transparent pricing, volume commitments, and optional revenue participation for the producer.
  • Rapid Deployment: Containerized units deployed in 45–90 days

Ideal Partner Profile

Stranded, associated, or flared gas with no current offtake agreement

Remote or off-grid well sites with 5 MW+ of recoverable gas energy

Regulatory pressure to reduce flaring or venting emissions

Interest in ESG-positive revenue diversification
