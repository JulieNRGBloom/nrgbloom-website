Global Team. Frontier Operations. Institutional Discipline.

NRG Bloom was founded in Canada and forged in Africa’s frontier energy markets. Our team combines deep operational experience in off-grid power systems with institutional-grade financial discipline. We launched with Bitcoin mining, first on Quebec’s hydroelectric grid, then on stranded gas in West Africa, because it is the fastest, lowest-risk way to prove that a remote energy site can sustain high-performance compute. That proof-of-concept methodology is now our competitive advantage as we scale into AI infrastructure globally.
