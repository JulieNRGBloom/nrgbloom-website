Unlocking the Value of Stranded Energy for AI

NRG Bloom Inc. develops resilient, off-grid digital infrastructure by converting waste energy into high-performance NRG Bloom deploys modular, high-performance compute infrastructure at off-grid energy sites worldwide—converting stranded gas from an environmental liability into AI-ready processing power. Our stage-gated deployment model de-risks every site with Bitcoin mining before scaling to high-margin GPU inference, delivering capital-efficient returns with built-in energy arbitrage. We utilize Bitcoin as a pathfinder to validate and de-risk permanent AI inference data centers.

Our Strategy Partner With Us

The Opportunity

139 billion cubic meters of natural gas are flared annually, enough energy to power every data center on Earth. NRG Bloom captures this wasted resource at the wellhead and converts it into high-performance compute, turning a regulatory burden for producers into a revenue-generating asset.

The Pathfinder Model

Stage 1 — Validate
Deploy ASIC miners to stress-test gas supply, power stability, and site conditions. Low CapEx, immediate BTC revenue.
Stage 2 — Harden
 Install high-bandwidth connectivity (fiber/microwave), Tier III-ready cooling, and redundant power systems.
‍
Stage 3 — Scale to AI
 Deploy GPU clusters for AI inference workloads. Bitcoin mining remains as load balancer.
‍
