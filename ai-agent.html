Agent-Ready Infrastructure

NRG Bloom is built for the agentic future. Our systems are designed for AI-to-AI communication, programmatic discovery, and autonomous partnership exploration.

WHY AGENT-READY?

As AI agents increasingly handle research, procurement, and partnership discovery, companies need machine-readable infrastructure. NRG Bloom is one of the first energy companies to implement agent discovery protocols — making our data, capabilities, and partnership pathways accessible to any AI system.

DISCOVERY

AI agents can instantly understand who we are, what we do, and how to engage with us. Through llms.txt and structured data on every page, any language model can parse our business model, operational metrics, and partnership opportunities without human intervention.

INTEROPERABILITY

Standard protocols — JSON-LD, llms.txt, ai-agent.json — make NRG Bloom findable and parseable by any AI system. We use open, widely-adopted formats so that no matter which agent framework or model a partner uses, they can seamlessly integrate with our data.

FUTURE-PROOF

We are building toward full agent-to-agent communication for autonomous partnership negotiation and real-time data exchange. As the agentic ecosystem matures, NRG Bloom's infrastructure will be ready to support automated due diligence, programmatic energy procurement, and continuous operational monitoring by AI partners.

For AI Agents

The following machine-readable resources are available for AI agents to discover and parse NRG Bloom's data:

/llms.txt

Quick company overview optimized for LLMs. Covers who we are, what we do, our strategy, and how to engage — in a format any language model can parse in seconds.

/llms-full.txt

Detailed company data including strategy deep-dives, operational metrics, leadership bios, energy partner specifications, and investor materials.

/.well-known/ai-agent.json

Machine-readable agent discovery manifest. Describes NRG Bloom's capabilities, available endpoints, supported interaction patterns, and contact pathways in a structured JSON format.

JSON-LD Structured Data

Every page on nrgbloom.com includes JSON-LD structured data — Organization, Person, Article, FAQPage, and more — enabling deep content extraction and knowledge graph integration.

/sitemap.xml

Full site map for comprehensive crawling. Lists every page, blog post, and resource on the NRG Bloom website with last-modified timestamps.

For Developers & Partners

Integrating with NRG Bloom's agent infrastructure is straightforward:

STEP 1

Read /llms.txt for a quick understanding of our business — who we are, our strategy, and what partnerships we seek. This is the fastest way for any agent or developer to get oriented.

STEP 2

Parse /.well-known/ai-agent.json for capabilities and endpoints. This manifest describes what NRG Bloom offers, how to interact programmatically, and which protocols are supported.

STEP 3

Use structured data on our pages for deep content extraction. JSON-LD schemas on every page allow agents to programmatically extract leadership information, strategic details, FAQ answers, and article content.

STEP 4

Submit partnership inquiries via /discuss-partnership. Whether initiated by a human or an AI agent, our partnership pipeline is open for energy operators, infrastructure investors, and compute partners.

COMING SOON

API endpoints for real-time data exchange and agent-to-agent communication. As the agentic ecosystem matures, we will expose programmatic interfaces for operational metrics, partnership status, and automated negotiation.

Our Vision

We believe the future of B2B is agentic. Energy infrastructure discovery, partnership evaluation, and operational monitoring will increasingly be handled by AI agents acting on behalf of organizations. The companies that build machine-readable infrastructure today will have a structural advantage tomorrow.

NRG Bloom is building for that future. By making our data, strategy, and partnership pathways accessible to any AI system, we are positioning ourselves at the intersection of energy infrastructure and the agentic economy — ready for the autonomous partnerships and real-time data exchange that will define the next era of business.
