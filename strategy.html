01
Stage One

Validate

60-90 day deployment

We deploy containerized ASIC mining units to candidate sites. These units act as a 24/7 operational stress test — proving whether the site can sustain high-value compute before we commit significant capital.

  • Gas quality and flow consistency verification
  • Generator uptime and thermal performance testing
  • Connectivity and remote monitoring stability
  • Regulatory and community alignment

Bitcoin mining is the ideal validation tool: zero customer SLA risk, zero latency sensitivity, and immediate revenue from day one.

Outcome: Sites that pass move to Stage 2. Sites that don’t are optimized for long-term Bitcoin mining — every location delivers value.
02
Stage Two

Harden

Infrastructure upgrade phase

Validated sites receive permanent infrastructure upgrades, transforming them from mining camps into certified compute facilities.

  • High-bandwidth connectivity via microwave links or fiber backhaul
  • Tier III-ready liquid or immersion cooling systems
  • Redundant power conditioning and UPS systems
  • Physical security and environmental monitoring

Bitcoin mining continues during this phase, generating revenue that partially offsets the CapEx of infrastructure hardening.

Outcome: A certified compute facility — not a temporary mining camp.
03
Stage Three

Scale to AI

GPU inference deployment

The hardened site receives GPU cluster deployments for AI inference workloads. Our Dynamic Load Orchestration system continuously allocates power between:

  • AI inference — high-margin, latency-sensitive, priority power allocation
  • Bitcoin mining — high-floor, always-on, absorbs 100% of off-peak capacity

Generators never idle. The PPA is always maximized. Bitcoin mining becomes the site’s “thermal battery” — absorbing excess energy, generating baseline revenue, and keeping cooling systems at optimal efficiency.

Outcome: A dual-revenue compute facility powered by energy that would otherwise be wasted.
